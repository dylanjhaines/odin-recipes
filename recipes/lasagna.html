Lasagna

This is a gluten free lasagna recipe!

Ingredients

  • GF Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Italian Sausage
  • Cheese!

Recipe

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Saute italian sausage
  3. Layer italian sausage, cheese, and noodles
  4. Repeat step 3 until the dish is full or you run out of ingredients
  5. Bake @375 until cheese bubbles
  6. Enjoy!
