Fajitas

This is a carne asada fajitas recipe!

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada (2 lbs)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ghee
  • Mexican Spices
  • Bell Peppers

Recipe

  1. Rub steak with ghee
  2. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and mexican spices liberally
  3. Cut steak and peppers into thin strips
  4. Saute peppers in ghee
  5. Sear steak
  6. Enjoy!
